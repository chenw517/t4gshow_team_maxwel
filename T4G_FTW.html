Future Work

Looking ahead, we envision powerful upgrades that will make our system smarter, more helpful, and more integrated into everyone's daily life.

Our ultimate goal is to create a platform which statifies the needs of our users. Everyone desires to indulge in scrumptious meals and be healthy at the same time.

Therefore, we intend to create a free and accessible platform for everyone. Besides, we want to become assistants that can provide helpful suggestions for users' in their daily decisions on what to cook and what to eat.

In order to do so, we plan to insert mass detectors, cameras and monitors into refrigerators as a way to monitor users' lifestyle and provide suggestions on what to purchase when shopping for groceries. Detailed plannings as a below:

1. Integration with Shopping Platforms

Connect our system with popular online grocery stores so that after AI suggests a recipe, users can instantly order missing ingredients online — saving time and making healthy cooking more accessible.

2. Mass Detection with Camera Integration

By linking cameras and weight sensors, the system can recognize how much food is taken from the fridge, enabling the AI to calculate accurate nutritional values and tailor recipes more precisely.

3. Smart Diet Monitoring System

A built-in smart system can monitor which items are regularly consumed, giving personalized advice on diet balance and generating shopping lists based on health and usage patterns.

4. Collaboration with Culinary Institutions

Work with cooking schools or students to create video guides and professional tutorials that teach users how to prepare each AI-recommended dish step-by-step.

5. Adaptation to Various Cooking Styles

Let AI learn users' favorite cooking styles — whether it's steaming, stir-frying, baking, or grilling — and offer personalized recipes that match their techniques and taste.

6. Professional Nutritionist Collaboration

Collaborate with certified nutritionists to ensure all AI-generated nutrition facts and advice are medically accurate, safe, and beneficial for all age groups.

7. Health Condition–Based Recipe Suggestions

Allow the system to consider users’ health needs — like diabetes or allergies — and provide recipe suggestions that align with their dietary restrictions and support their well-being.
