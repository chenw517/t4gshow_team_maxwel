Harvard plate

The Harvard Plate is a visual guide for balanced meals. It recommends filling:
1/2 the plate with vegetables and fruits,
1/4 with whole grains,
1/4 with healthy protein like fish, beans, or nuts.
It also encourages using healthy oils and drinking water instead. It's designed to promote long-term health and prevent chronic diseases.
